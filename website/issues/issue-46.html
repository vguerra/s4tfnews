S4TF News - Issue 46, January 26th 2020

Welcome to the Issue #46 of Swift for TensorFlow Newsletter. A newsletter letter that summarizes weekly activity around the Swift for TensorFlow project.

Enjoy!

News and Articles

  • On Friday 24th of January, the 22nd SIG Swift Design meeting took place. Here you have the highlights:

    • Jacopo Mangiavacchi gave a lightning talk based on his recent post on exporting Swift for TensorFlow models into CoreML. He has made his slides and code available.

    • Sylvain Gugger continuing the descussions on input pipelines, he gave a tour of Batcher.

    You can find the meeting recording, notes and all questions raised in this google doc.

S4TF News - Issue 86, November 1st 2020

Welcome to the Issue #86 of Swift for TensorFlow Newsletter. A newsletter letter that summarizes weekly activity around the Swift for TensorFlow project.

Enjoy!

News and Articles

  • A new tutorial is available that guides you through the parts of Differentiable Swift that are not so obvious ... yet. It is available at Sharp edges in Differentiable Swift.

  • Brett Koonce gave an introduction talk on Swift for TensorFlow during GDG DevFest Cloud Kampala, Uganda. The slides and recording of the talk are now available online.

  • New Development snapshots are available for you to try out Swift for Tensorflow: Windows.

Swift Models

    • PR-692: Repairs Windows build for WordSeg.

S4TF News - Issue 36, November 17th 2019

Welcome to the Issue #36 of Swift for TensorFlow Newsletter. A newsletter letter that summarizes weekly activity around the Swift for TensorFlow project.

Enjoy!

News and Articles

  • On Friday 15th of November, the 16th SIG Swift Design meeting took place. Here you have the highlights:

    • Marc Rasi presented a tool called ADPropertyTests that he authored to Randomly generate Test cases for Swift Automatic DIfferentiation. The design document for the tool is available here and you can as well checkout the tool's source code on GitHub.

    You can find the meeting notes and all questions raised in this google doc.

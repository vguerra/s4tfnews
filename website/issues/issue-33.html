S4TF News - Issue 33, October 27th 2019

Welcome to the Issue #33 of Swift for TensorFlow Newsletter. A newsletter letter that summarizes weekly activity around the Swift for TensorFlow project.

Enjoy!

News and Articles

  • Sanyam Bhutani has released, this last weekend, two interviews on his Chai Time Data Science podcast that you should listen to:

    • An interview with Dr. Marc Lanctot where they talk all about Research at DeepMind, Deep Learning Research, AlphaGo, Swift For TensorFlow and OpenSpiel. Check out the video on Youtube.

    • An interview with Bart Chrzaszcz where they talk about his Google Software Engineering Internship, his contributions to Swift For TensorFlow and OpenSpiel. Check out the video on Youtube.

  • The upstreaming effort to bring differentiable programming to the master branch of swift repository has started. All PRs related are prefixed with [AutoDiff upstream].

  • Paige Bailey was at try! Swift NYC presenting Swift for TensorFlow. You can find the recording of her talk, Swift for TensorFlow - Machine Learning with no Boundaries, in Youtube.

  • On Friday 25th of October, the 14th SIG Swift Design meeting took place. Here you have the highlights:

    • Edward Connell presented SwiftRT: A Tensor runtime layer to meet the high performance requirements of Swift for TensorFlow. On this 1st part of the presentation Edward went into the internals of the library. Stay tunned for the 2nd part.

    • Dan Zheng has prorposed that all additions or changes to the API should be discussed before merging into the swift-apis repo. Among the recent changes we have:

      • PR-529: TensorFlow checkpoint reader.
      • PR-319: Layer parameter initializers.

    • Last but not least, Dan Zheng presented the upcoming retroactive derivative registration, which will allow for definition of derivatives in different modules.

    You can find the meeting notes and all questions raised in this google doc.

S4TF Commits

Swift Compiler (tensorflow branch)

  • PR-27861: Replaces -Xllvm -run-jvp-generation by -enable-experimental-forward-mode-differentiation to enable forward-mode differentiation. Resolves TF-935.

  • PR-27854: Adds support for declaration-only SILDifferentiabilityWitness.

  • PR-27850: Fixes memory bug in IndexSubset.

  • PR-27726: Fixes a bug on IRGen for invalid IR emited for some partial_apply instructions. Resolves TF-917.

Swift TensorFlow APIs

  • PR-536: Makes empty tangent vector initializer public.

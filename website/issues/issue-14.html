S4TF Commits

Swift Compiler (tensorflow branch)

  • PR-25500: Introduces ElementaryFunctions protocol and derives conformances to it for struct types. Adds ability to derive ElementaryFunctions conformances for struct type.

  • PR-25495: Fixes a crash on AdditiveArithmetic derived conformances due to missing memberwise initializers.

  • PR-24786: Makes SIMD vectors differentiable.

  • PR-25437: Changes Differentiable.moved(along:) to mutating func move(along:).

  • PR-25325: Adds initial support to @differentiable(linear) types.

  • PR-25381: Substitutes VectorProtocol scalar multiplication operators with VectorProtocol.scaled(by:).

  • PR-25374: Renames Rename VectorProtocol.Scalar to VectorSpaceScalar.

  • PR-25371: Renames protocol VectorNumeric to VectorProtocol. Removes as well ShapedVectorNumeric protocol.

  • PR-25368: Fixes diagnostics on fulfilled @differentiable attribute.

  • PR-25339: Improves importing nested types and namespaces for C++ interoperability.

  • PR-25324: Fixes conformance checking in @differentiable attribute's wrt: parameter.

  • PR-25277: Adds diagnostic for when in @differentiable attribute, linear is specified together with and vjp: or jvp:.

  • PR-25293: Improves compiler diagnostics in case of duplicated attribute @differentiable.

Swift TensorFlow APIs

  • Introduced Losses and Error functions:

    • PR-240: Introduces Cosine Distance cosineDistance(x:,y:).

    • PR-228: Introduces Mean Absolute Percentage error: meanAbsolutePercentageError(predicted:,expected:).

    • PR-233: Introduces Cosine Similarity cosineSimilarity(predicted:expected:).

    • PR-226: Introduces Kullback-Leibler divergence kullbackLeiblerDivergence(predicted:,expected:).

    • PR-231: Introduces Adding L1 & L2 Losses: l1Loss(predicted:expected:), l2Loss(predicted:expected:).

    • PR-221: Introduces poissonLoss(predicted:expected:).

    • PR-193: Introduces meanSquaredLogarithmicError(predicted:expected:).

  • PR-225: Introduces softplus and softsign.

  • PR-198: Introduces clipped(min: Tensor, max: Tensor) for Tensor.

  • PR-200: Makes Tensor conform to ElementaryFunctions.

  • Applying changes and renamings:

    • PR-236: Applying change of Differentiable.moved(along:) to move(along:).

    • PR-207: Applies renaming of VectorProtocol.Scalar to VectorSpaceScalar.

    • PR-205: Applies renaming of VectorNumeric to VectorProtocol.

  • PR-214: Adds the following Tensor operators: acos, asin, atan, acosh, asinh, atanh, log2, log10, exp2, exp10, pow(Tensor, Int), root(Tensor, Int).

  • PR-203: Adds LazyTensor.forEachOperation.

  • PR-192: Introduces AdaGrad optimizer.

  • Improvements to pretty printing of LazyTensorOperation:

    • PR-206: Print LazyTensorOperation in the form "%id = op...".
    • PR-196: Handling concrete values when pretty-priting LazyTensorOperations.

  • PR-190: Adds ability to track the liveness of LazyTensors.

  • PR-184: Adds derivative for padded(forSizes:with:).

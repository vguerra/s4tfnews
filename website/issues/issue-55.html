S4TF News - Issue 55, March 29th 2020

Welcome to the Issue #55 of Swift for TensorFlow Newsletter. A newsletter letter that summarizes weekly activity around the Swift for TensorFlow project.

Enjoy!

News and Articles

    • Saleem Abdulrasool gave an overview of the different build systems used within the Swift for TensorFlow ecosystem. You can find his slides online.

S4TF Commits

Swift Compiler (tensorflow branch)

  • PR-30647: Removes deprecated attribute @nondiff.

  • PR-30622: Stops the handling of curry levels in builtin. Resolves SR-11852.

  • PR-30623: Separates ring math Protocol derived conformances.

  • PR-30613: Adds preset for x10 with GPU.

  • PR-30600: Removes swift-tensorflow-quotes from update-checkout configuration.

  • PR-30002: Forbids derivative registration using @differentiable. Resolves TF-1001.

Swift TensorFlow APIs

  • PR-783: Favors absl::StrAppend instead of absl::StrCat for appending to a string.

  • PR-767: Fixes bug in batchGathering.

  • PR-756: Refactors and improves error messages in preconditions.

  • PR-782: Adds fallback generation for some XLA ops that are missing implementations.

  • PR-693: Renames recurrent layers: Protocol RNNCell becomes RecurrentLayerCell, SimpleRNNCell becomes BasicRNNCell, RNN Layer becomes RecurrentLayer.

  • PR-774: Adds runtime dispatching.

  • PR-778: Adds stubs for 10x raw operations depthToSpace and spaceToDepth.

  • PR-710: Introduces depthToSpace and spaceToDepth operations. Partially resolves Issue-685

Swift Models

  • PR-415: Replaces _Raw resizing operations in FastStyleTransfer model . Partially resolves Issue-225.

  • PR-424: Fixes output shape of Image.resized.

  • PR-418: Removes all usages of Dataset in favor of Batcher.

  • PR-417: Prevents Datasets from re-downloading, they are downloaded into ~/.s4tf/datasets. Resolves Issue-381.

  • PR-376: Implements C3D model for video action classification.

  • PR-344: Implements the DLRM (Deep Learning Recommendation Model for Personalization and Recommendation Systems) model.

  • PR-395: Replaces deprecated substring operation on strings.

  • PR-413: Moves Transformer into Examples/GPT2-Inference. Resolves Issue-387.

  • PR-414: Improvements to FastStyleTransfer Colab demo notebook. Resolves Issue-410.

  • PR-357: . Resolves Issue-354.Uses ArgumentParser to parse CLI options for Benchmarks target.

Swift Jupyter

  • PR-93: Initial support for successful registration of the swift kernel in Windows.

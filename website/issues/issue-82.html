S4TF News - Issue 82, October 4th 2020

Welcome to the Issue #82 of Swift for TensorFlow Newsletter. A newsletter letter that summarizes weekly activity around the Swift for TensorFlow project.

Enjoy!

News and Articles

  • On Friday 2nd of October, the 41st SIG Swift Design meeting took place. Here you have the highlights:

    • Vojta Molda gave an in-depth walkthrough of his Differentiable shallow water PDE solver PR. Slides available here.

    • Brad Larson went over one of his recent PRs: a Growing neural cellular automata (PR). A notebook showcasing the implementation is available here.

    You can find the notes for this meeting in this google doc.

    Recording from this session is available online as well.

  • New Development snapshots are available for you to try out Swift for Tensorflow: Ubuntu 18.04 - CPU, Nightly with CUDA 11.0 support.

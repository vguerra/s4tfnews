## S4TF Commits

#### Swift Compiler (tensorflow branch)

* [PR-28945](https://github.com/apple/swift/pull/28945): Fixes macOS toolchains with Xcode new build system.

* [PR-28890](https://github.com/apple/swift/pull/28890): Forbids derivatives on protocol requirements.

* [PR-28932](https://github.com/apple/swift/pull/28932): Deprecates `@differentiable(jvp:vjp:)` arguments. 

* [PR-28927](https://github.com/apple/swift/pull/28927): Fixes `@differentiable(linear)` type-checking. 

#### Swift TensorFlow APIs

* [PR-592](https://github.com/tensorflow/swift-apis/pull/592): Adds `logdet` operation . Partially solves [Issue-592](https://github.com/tensorflow/swift-apis/pull/592).

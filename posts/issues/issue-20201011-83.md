

S4TF News - Issue 83, October 11th 2020
===================

Welcome to the Issue #83 of *Swift for TensorFlow Newsletter*. A newsletter letter that summarizes weekly activity around the Swift for TensorFlow project.

Enjoy!

---

## S4TF Commits

#### Swift TensorFlow APIs

* [PR-1102](https://github.com/tensorflow/swift-apis/pull/1102): Converts device to a pointer instead of a string.

* [PR-1100](https://github.com/tensorflow/swift-apis/pull/1100): Favors using `absl::node_hash_map` instead of `std::unordered_map`.

* [PR-1097](https://github.com/tensorflow/swift-apis/pull/1097): Converts `topk`, `svd`, `qr`, `where`, `sum`, and `convs` to have type information.

#### Swift Models

* [PR-672](https://github.com/tensorflow/swift-models/pull/672): Adds an interface for toggling live statistics in `TrainingLoop`.
